Our Mission

Pay it Forward strives to provide equitable access to STEM education for all K-8 students, breaking barriers and creating opportunities for everyone to contribute to advancements in STEM fields.

Research-Based Approach

We combine our individual research experiences to deliver a novel research-based approach to teaching STEM subjects for all age groups.

Equity & Inclusion

We're committed to breaking barriers and ensuring equitable access to quality STEM education for students from all backgrounds.

Global Impact

Through our partnership in Uganda, we help children with limited access to education receive responsible instruction in STEM subjects.

Our Uganda Partnership

We're proud to partner with schools in Uganda to provide STEM education to children with limited access to educational resources.

Making a Global Impact

Our partnership in Uganda focuses on providing responsible STEM education to children who have limited access to educational resources. Through virtual and in-person programs, we're helping to build a foundation for future innovation.

By sharing our curriculum and resources, we aim to inspire a new generation of STEM enthusiasts and future scientists, engineers, and innovators in communities that have traditionally been underserved.
